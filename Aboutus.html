About us

Our motto is a Healthy nation!

During the work of Cfib "Strong" has established itself not only as a club of high level of sports services, but also as a club available to everyone. Cfib "Strong" has its national teams in: powerlifting, arm wrestling, bodybuilding, Thai Boxing, regularly holds sports events to promote a healthy lifestyle, promote and develop sports in the Kyrgyz Republic, the formation of a club team to participate in the Asian and World Championships. Cfib "Strong" in its territory has spacious, equipped rooms with access to fresh air and sunlight, which are located on three levels. Separate locker rooms for each room, individual turnkey wardrobe for training, showers with a large supply of hot and cold water. At Your service the most modern equipment, highly qualified coaching staff, nutritionists, high level of service, flexible schedule of visit of trainings and individual approach to everyone. All gyms are equipped with professional fitness equipment, leading manufacturers Vision Fitness, Johnson, Power Max, Matrix, etc., meeting the highest standards. The cardio area of the hall offers classes on bicycles, treadmills, elliptical, rowing and other simulators that contribute to the development of General endurance and strengthen the cardiovascular system. The free weights area in the gyms of The cfib "Strong" offers a wide range of professional sports equipment and accessories used in the fitness industry. Specially designed training programs in the gym with free weight, using barbells and dumbbells help to shape the relief of the body, maintain muscle tone and always be in shape! The priority direction of gyms is fitness training for figure correction and health and Wellness program. With the latest innovative workouts, beginners have the opportunity to develop strength, endurance, flexibility, reaction and body balance during individual and semi-personal workouts. After a workout, you have the opportunity to relax in the cozy phyto-bar, which for your convenience is located in the upper and lower levels, as well as restore strength, using the services of a massage therapist.

For your convenience, the complex is open from 7.00 to 23.00.
